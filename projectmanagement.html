Project Manager - Mobile Development

Mobile application development is the set of processes and procedures involved in writing software for small, wireless computing devices such as smartphones or tablets.


Most common expectation of all projects that are initiated is to achieve the objectives defined. In order to complete the projects successfully, scope, schedule, cost and quality have to be planned and tracked closely. Human resources, vendors and other stakeholders involved in the project have to be managed. Any potential risks in completing project activities have to be managed as well. Providing timely updates and reporting the relevant project status to the right stakeholders to enable decision making is one of the key success factor.


    A Certified Mobile Development Project Manager must be able to demonstrate skills to perform the following:
  • Review and align project objectives to meet strategic objectives of the organization
  • Identify stakeholders and conduct project kick-off meeting to involve relevant stakeholders in the project
  • Develop project management plan, which describes plan for scope, schedule, cost, quality, vendor management, quality, risk, human resource, stakeholders and communication
  • Execute all the planned activities by allocating resources to achieve project objectives with a target to complete the project on time and within budget
  • Monitor project progress and report project status to top management and relevant stakeholders on periodic basis as planned
  • Take appropriate corrective and preventive actions to manage variations and address issues, if any
  • Obtain sign-off from the client once all the deliverables are delivered
  • Conduct retrospective meeting to consolidate the lessons learnt during the project
